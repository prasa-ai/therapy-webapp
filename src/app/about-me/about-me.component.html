Professional Experience & Approach

With over 12 years of clinical and case management experience, I help individuals move through anxiety, depression, trauma, life transitions, and identity questions with clarity and purpose. My clients often come to me during moments of crisis or change, looking for insight, grounding, and sustainable paths forward.

My approach is person-centered, collaborative, and informed by a diverse professional background that includes forensic psychology, legal systems, crisis intervention, and community-based care. I have experience working with serious mental illness, dual diagnoses, and individuals navigating complex life systems, including disability services, court processes, and social supports.

In addition to therapy, I also specialize in comprehensive immigration evaluations and career counseling, blending psychological insight with deep knowledge of systems. Whether you're in immediate crisis or facing long-term questions about direction, relationships, or meaning, I provide a compassionate, structured space to explore what matters most to you.
